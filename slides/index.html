0: Set up your Raspberry Pi

Time to plug everything in!

  1. Insert Micro SD Card into underside of Raspbery Pi
  2. Plug HDMI cable into the monitor and Raspbery Pi (use left HDMI on Pi)
  3. Plug Keyboard and mouse into Raspbery PI
  4. Put the GPIO leaf over the GPIO pins
  5. Plug the Power supply
